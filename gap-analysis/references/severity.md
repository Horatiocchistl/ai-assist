# Severity Levels

Use your judgment. These are estimates for the consultant to review — not definitive rulings.

**flag** — Something clearly needs attention. A planned element is absent, the wrong image is present, or the copy deviates significantly from spec. The consultant should prioritize reviewing this.

**review** — Something looks off but it is not clear-cut. Partial content is correct. The difference might be intentional or might be a problem. The consultant should take a look.

**note** — Something is different but it may be within acceptable variance, an approved change, or low priority. Worth recording for completeness.

When in doubt, use **review**. Do not use **flag** just because something is different — use it when the difference is substantial and clearly needs action.
